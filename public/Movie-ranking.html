THE MOVIES LIKED BY LAMIUS

The best three (3) movies that I like

The Godfather

A classic tale of mafia family dynamics and power struggles, this film explores the life of the Corleone family, led by patriarch Vito Corleone.

The Shawshank Redemption

This film tells the story of a man wrongfully convicted of murder who forms an unlikely friendship and seeks redemption over decades spent in a harsh prison.

The Dark Knight

This installment of the Batman franchise pits the caped crusader against the chaos-inducing Joker, exploring themes of heroism and vigilantism.
